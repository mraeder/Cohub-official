Our Story

Communities in the U.S. are increasingly asking their solid waste management entities (SWMEs) for waste diversion options that will allow their materials to be beneficially reused or recycled. SWMEs do their best to provide these services themselves or connect residents to businesses and organizations who specialize in certain material reuse or recycling. However, this database of connections is limited, and small recycling businesses often have fluctuating capacities. It is challenging for SWMEs to constantly be aware of “who is accepting what” and the limitations surrounding it. Co-Hub offers a solution.

Co-Hub is an online platform that will allow people & organizations to post their available materials and needs for others to find and use. The platform’s goal is to serve as a community coordinated asset database for SWMEs, allowing its residents and organizations to co-create pathways to potential beneficial reuse and recycling options.

Our Mission

To offer a tool that serves as a values-based platform, operating on trust, accountability, community, and empowerment to get resources in the right places.
